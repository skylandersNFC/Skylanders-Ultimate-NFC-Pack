Template Template is used as a debug character and can be found in various Vicarious Visions titles, first seen in Skylanders: Swap Force. He is a humanoid character with no particular details aside from large hands and a tail. He cant deal damage and can be swapped with other swappers.

*Don't use the top half on Imaginators, he just immediately crashes the game.*
