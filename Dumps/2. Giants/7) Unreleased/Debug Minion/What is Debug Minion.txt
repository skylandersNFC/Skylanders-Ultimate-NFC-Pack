Debug Minion is a debug character and can be found in the Wii U version of Skylanders Spyro's Adventure and the Wii U and Xbox 360 versions of Skylanders Giants. It's Magic Moment and stats screen show it as a coin whilst in game it uses Whirlwind's model and animations, It has the ability to instantly kill an enemy and when it wears a hat, it is very over-sized.
